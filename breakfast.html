Ful Medames:

Recipe: Soak dried fava beans overnight. Boil the beans until tender. In a separate pan, sauté onions, garlic, and tomatoes until soft. Add the cooked beans, along with salt, cumin, and paprika. Mash some of the beans to thicken the sauce. Serve with injera or bread.

  • Tips: You can add a squeeze of lemon juice and garnish with chopped parsley for added freshness and flavor.
  • Spices: Cumin, paprika.

Chechebsa:

Recipe: Make a dough by mixing flour, salt, and water. Roll out the dough into thin circles. In a pan, heat oil and fry the flatbread until cooked. Tear the cooked flatbread into bite-sized pieces. In a separate pan, sauté onions, garlic, and berbere spice. Add the torn flatbread and mix well. Serve warm.

  • Tips: You can garnish with chopped tomatoes, jalapenos, and a drizzle of niter kibbeh (spiced clarified butter) for added flavor.
  • Spices: Berbere spice.

Kita Firfir:

Recipe: In a bowl, mix together flour, baking powder, salt, and water. Heat oil in a pan and pour a thin layer of the batter. Cook until both sides are golden brown. Once cooled, tear the cooked flatbread into small pieces. In a separate pan, sauté onions, garlic, and tomatoes. Add the torn flatbread and mix well. Season with salt and pepper. Serve warm.

  • Tips: You can add sliced green chilies for an extra kick.
  • Spices: Salt, pepper.

Kinche:

Recipe: Cook cracked wheat in water until tender. In a separate pan, sauté onions, garlic, and tomatoes until soft. Add the cooked cracked wheat and mix well. Season with salt and pepper. Serve hot.

  • Tips: You can top it with a dollop of niter kibbeh or a drizzle of olive oil for added richness.
  • Spices: Salt, pepper.

Fit-fit:

Recipe: Toast pieces of injera in a dry pan until crispy. In another pan, sauté onions, garlic, and tomatoes until softened. Break the toasted injera into small pieces and add it to the pan. Mix well and season with salt, pepper, and spices like turmeric or paprika. Cook for a few more minutes until heated through. Serve warm.

  • Tips: You can add cooked lentils or leftover meat for added protein and flavor.
  • Spices: Turmeric, paprika, salt, pepper.

Genfo:

Recipe: In a saucepan, bring water to a boil. Slowly add finely ground barley or wheat flour while stirring continuously to avoid lumps. Cook over low heat, stirring constantly until the mixture thickens. Add a pinch of salt and butter. Serve warm with a drizzle of niter kibbeh (spiced clarified butter) or honey.

  • Tips: You can sprinkle ground cinnamon or cardamom on top for added aroma and flavor.
  • Spices: None (unless adding optional spices like cinnamon or cardamom).
